FAQ

  • What does SloppifAI do?
    It uses on-device AI to transform your photos into gloriously wrong AI slop. Extra fingers, melting faces, uncanny valley portraits — all generated locally on your phone.
  • Are my photos uploaded anywhere?
    No. All processing runs on your device using Apple's Neural Engine. Nothing leaves your phone.
  • Why does it take a while to generate?
    The AI model runs entirely on your phone's hardware. Generation typically takes 15–45 seconds depending on your device.
  • Why do results look different every time?
    Each generation uses a random seed, so you'll never get the same slop twice — even from the same photo.
  • Which devices are supported?
    Any iPhone with an A12 chip or later (iPhone XS and newer).
  • The app downloaded a large file on first launch. What was that?
    SloppifAI downloads the AI model (~2 GB) once on first launch. After that, everything runs offline.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

On-Device Processing. SloppifAI transforms photos using Apple's CoreML Stable Diffusion framework. All image processing runs entirely on your device using the Neural Engine. No photos or image data are transmitted to any server.

Face & Biometric Data. SloppifAI does not collect, detect, analyze, or store face data or any biometric information. The app does not use facial recognition, face detection, or any facial analysis APIs. Photos are processed as a whole image — no facial features are isolated, identified, extracted, or mapped. No face data is shared with any third party. No face data is retained because none is collected.

Photo Storage. Generated images are stored only in the app's local, sandboxed storage on your device. You may optionally save results to your Camera Roll or share them via the standard iOS Share Sheet. Images are retained locally until you delete them from the in-app gallery. No images are uploaded to or backed up on any external server.

Model Download. On first launch, the app downloads AI model files (~2 GB) from Hugging Face. This is a one-time download of model weights only — no user data is sent during this process.

Advertising. SloppifAI uses Google AdMob to display ads. The AdMob SDK may collect standard advertising identifiers, coarse location, and device information for ad serving and measurement, as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You may opt out of personalized ads through the consent prompt shown on first launch.

Third-Party Data Sharing. SloppifAI does not share any user photos or personal data with third parties. The only third-party data sharing is through the Google AdMob SDK as described above.
